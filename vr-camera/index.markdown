# Recording

## Must-haves

* Stereoscopy
* Correct IPD
* High Framerate (60+)
* Wide FOV

## 360 Video

* Pros:
   * Greater immersion
* Cons:
   * Loss of creative control
   * 3D is hard/impossible
   * Ton of equipment

## Stereoscopic video

* Pros:
   * Simple/familiar
* Cons:
   * Smaller FOV
   * Camera movement

## {data-background="images/gopro-dual-hero.jpg"}

## GoPro 3

* 1080p60
* 114 degree horizontal FOV
   * 16:9 hurts vertical FOV
* GoPro 4 has 4:3 1440p 80fps

## IPD

* Inter-Pupilar Distance
* Informs Scale
* Priority #1

## IPD

* Stock GoPro 30cm
* Inverted GoPro 90cm
* Needed ~64cm IPD
